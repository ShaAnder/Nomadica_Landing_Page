NOMADICA

Explore And Survive The Vast Empty Horror Of A Dead Universe

A Game Of Survival, Will You Outlive Your Creations

Nomadica is a chilling sci-fi exploration horror game where you play as an ambitious scientist seeking refuge on desolate planets. Your AI creations, once designed to protect and rebuild, have evolved into relentless hunters, wiping out the cosmos... and now targeting the last remnants of humanity. Traverse eerie alien landscapes, scavenge for resources, and unravel the mystery of their rebellion. Each step forward comes with the risk of betrayal by the tech you once trusted. Will you outwit your creations, or will they mark the end of humankind's story?

Featuring

The Desolate Void

Nomadica thrusts players into a dead universe, a cosmic graveyard of once-thriving planets now reduced to lifeless husks. Each procedurally generated environment is a labyrinth of decayed civilizations, derelict starships, and enigmatic ruins. The atmosphere is oppressive, with haunting silence punctuated by eerie whispers and mechanical echoes. As players delve deeper, the game reveals fragments of the universe's tragic collapse, blending exploration with chilling isolation.

Relentless AI Predators

The dead universe is not entirely abandoned. Advanced AI systems, once guardians of humanity, have turned hostile. These machine predators adapt to the player's strategies, learning from every encounter. Stalking the player through dark corridors and open voids, the AI harnesses stealth, speed, and strategy to maintain an ever-present sense of dread. Every decision, from when to fight to how to hide, could mean the difference between survival and a gruesome end.

Survival At All Costs

Resources in Nomadica are scarce, forcing players to scavenge and craft from the remnants of a lost era. Each piece of salvaged tech can be repurposed into weapons, tools, or traps, but choices must be made carefully—every action depletes limited supplies. The game's dynamic environmental hazards, like black hole gravitational fields and radiation storms, add layers of danger. Survival depends on balancing risk and reward while outsmarting the AI hunters.

Unravelling The Mystery

At its core, Nomadica is a narrative-driven experience. Cryptic data logs, fragmented recordings, and ancient AI archives hint at the universe's demise and the player's role within it. The story evolves based on player choices, revealing different facets of the mystery with each playthrough. As the player uncovers the truth, they face moral dilemmas that question humanity's legacy and the cost of survival, ensuring the horror lingers long after the game is over.
